# Support

Welcome to the digital atelier of Tathastu Naranje. 

This repository is a curated collection of thought—spanning market intelligence frameworks, speculative fiction concepts, and philosophical essays. It is designed as a resource for the curious and the strategic.

## Seeking Guidance

Given the nature of this content, "support" is less about troubleshooting code and more about intellectual engagement and clarity.

If you require assistance or wish to discuss the material:

1.  **Consult the Material:** Ensure you have thoroughly reviewed the relevant essay or framework. Often, the nuance is embedded within the text itself.
2.  **Open an Issue:** For questions regarding the application of a market intelligence framework, or for requests to clarify a philosophical concept presented in an essay, please open an Issue. 
    *   Frame your question clearly.
    *   Provide context for your inquiry.
    *   Maintain a tone of constructive discourse.
3.  **Authentic Data:** If your inquiry relates to the data underpinning a framework, please note that all insights are derived from authentic, verifiable sources. If you have contradictory data, please present it with rigorous citation.

## Response Expectations

I review inquiries periodically. As a consultant and creator, my time is divided among various strategic and artistic pursuits. I will endeavor to respond to thoughtful, well-articulated questions as time permits. 

This space is an ongoing dialogue. Your engagement is appreciated.
